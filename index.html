      • Synopsis

      Raito was perfectly content just cruising though his student years and enjoying his limited time with a close friend or two. However, after the end of the last summer break, he couldn't help but notice that his best friends started to feel distant and preferred to associate with the stranger personalities of the school. Worried for their well-being, he began to track their activities only to find that reality as he knows it no longer fully applies. On top of that, each of these "weirdos" happened to all be involved with something much greater than the world he lives in; whether it be students or even school staff. In this newly discovered chaos, Raito grasped at an opportunity to see for himself how the many realities of the multiverse intertwine. All he needed at first is a Covenant...
